## Commands Details

{% hint style="info" %}
`<>` means optional.  
`[]` means required.  
Don't include them in your commands.
{% endhint %}

### $avatar

Description: _Shows the avatar of the user, mentioned user or provided ID._  
Usage: `$avatar <user>`
